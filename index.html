Factors to Consider When Choosing an Renovation Contractor Singapore

These are 5 things to consider when hiring an interior designer in Singapore.

Accreditation: Has the interior design firm been professionally accredited?

You may be able to find a professional interior designer if this is your first job. There are many interior design licenses available in Singapore, including the HDB license.

An HDB-licensed contractor is one who has been through extensive training and is familiar with the regulations for renovations in public housing. The accreditation also guarantees that interior designers are licensed.

  • Has at least three years' experience in the industry and is active in the renovation trade
  • ACRA-registered at least for 1 year
  • Has good records

Budget: What amount are you willing and able to spend on this project?

You should establish a realistic budget based on the project's size and complexity and talk about it with potential interior designers.

Interior designers will work with any budget to maximize your investment. This means that even if you are only looking at a certain price range, you can be sure you will get the best out of your interior designer.

Experience: How long have the interior designers been in the business?

Although it is fine to hire an inexperienced interior designer, they are more likely be able to provide a higher quality service. They have the necessary skills and equipment to fulfill your renovation needs.

While experience is important, it's equally important to evaluate the business reputation. Online reviews can help you find out what past clients have to say about the designer you are considering hiring.

Style: Is their style similar to yours?

It is also important to consider the design style and processes of potential interior designers when designing a space, such as minimalist interior design. Each interior designer has their own style and it is important to ensure that you and your design preferences are compatible.

It's a great idea to get to know at least three potential designers to get a sense of their work styles and personalities. It will be much easier to compare their styles and choose the one that is more similar to you.

Timeline: Are they able to complete the work within a reasonable amount of time?

Start by identifying the time you want the project completed and then find an interior designer who will be able to give you a realistic timeline.

You should expect delays and issues during the process, but a good interior designer should still be able finish within the deadline. If there are any delays or problems, the company should be able inform you so that you are kept informed.

It doesn't matter how well-respected an interior designer may be or how confident you are in their abilities and experience. If you don't communicate your needs clearly, then it won’t matter.
